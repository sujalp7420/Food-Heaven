Mocktails Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 lime (cut into wedges)
  • 1 cup soda or sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Muddle mint leaves, lime wedges, and sugar in a glass.
  2. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour soda over it and mix well.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 1 tablespoon blue curaçao syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add blue curaçao syrup and lemonade.
  3. Stir well and garnish with a lemon slice.
  4. Serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Pineapple slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Blend pineapple juice, coconut milk, sugar, and ice cubes until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass.
  3. Garnish with a pineapple slice.
  4. Serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lemon-lime soda
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Maraschino cherry for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour lemon-lime soda over the ice.
  3. Add grenadine syrup and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and serve.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries (blended)
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Blend strawberries with lemon juice and sugar.
  2. Add cold water and mix well.
  3. Pour over ice cubes in a glass.
  4. Serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh watermelon juice
  • 1/2 cup coconut water
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Blend fresh watermelon and strain to get the juice.
  2. Pour the watermelon juice into a glass filled with ice cubes.
  3. Add coconut water and mix well.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup apple juice
  • 1/2 cup soda water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Apple slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour apple juice and lemon juice into the glass.
  3. Top up with soda water and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with an apple slice and serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup cranberry juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour orange juice and then slowly add cranberry juice to create a layered effect.
  3. Garnish with an orange slice.
  4. Serve chilled.
