Appetizers & Snacks Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring roll sheets
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix all vegetables with soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place a spoonful of the mixture in a spring roll sheet and roll tightly.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with sweet chili sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Hakka noodles
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, capsicum, beans)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles as per package instructions, drain, and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic, and sauté vegetables.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss in noodles and stir well.
  5. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup paneer cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon red chili sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Fry paneer cubes until golden and set aside.
  2. Heat oil, sauté garlic, onions, and bell peppers.
  3. Add sauces and mix well.
  4. Toss in fried paneer, coat well, and serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread crumbs for coating
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix potatoes, corn, cheese, cornflour, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape into balls, coat with breadcrumbs.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with ketchup.

Ingredients:

  • 10 button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Remove mushroom stems and chop them.
  2. Sauté chopped stems with onions and garlic.
  3. Mix with cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff mixture into mushrooms and bake at 180°C for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for grilling

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients into a dough.

  • 1 cup paneer cubes

  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup baby corn
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix baby corn with flour, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Deep fry until crispy.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 1 teaspoon oregano

Instructions:

  1. Mix butter with garlic and spread on bread.
  2. Sprinkle cheese and oregano.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • 6 chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:
