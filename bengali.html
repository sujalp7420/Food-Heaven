Ingredients:

  • Potatoes, cubed
  • Poppy seeds paste
  • Green chilies, slit
  • Mustard oil
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Fry potatoes lightly.

Ingredients:

  • Hilsa fish pieces
  • Mustard seeds paste
  • Green chilies, slit
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Marinate fish with turmeric and salt.
  2. Mix mustard paste with turmeric and salt.
  3. Layer fish and mustard mixture in a pan.
  4. Add green chilies and mustard oil.
  5. Cover and cook until done.
  6. Serve with rice.

Ingredients:

  • Chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • Coconut, grated
  • Ghee
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cardamom pods
  • Bay leaf
  • Raisins
  • Cashews
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Cook chana dal.
  2. Temper spices in ghee.
  3. Add coconut, raisins, and cashews.
  4. Add cooked dal and sugar.
  5. Simmer and serve.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Yogurt culture
  • Sugar

Instructions:

  1. Caramelize sugar.
  2. Boil milk and add caramelized sugar.
  3. Cool slightly and add yogurt culture.
  4. Set in earthen pots and let it set.
  5. Chill before serving.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Knead flour with water and salt.
  2. Make small balls and roll into small circles.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot.
