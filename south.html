  • Rice
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter.
  3. Ferment overnight.
  4. Spread batter on a hot griddle.
  5. Cook until crispy.
  6. Serve with sambar and chutney.

Ingredients:

  • Toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  • Tamarind extract
  • Vegetables (drumstick, brinjal, etc.)
  • Sambar powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves

Instructions:

  1. Cook toor dal.
  2. Add vegetables and tamarind extract.
  3. Add sambar powder and simmer.
  4. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  5. Serve with idli or dosa.

Ingredients:

  • Semolina (rava)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Urad dal (black gram)
  • Curry leaves
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas)

Instructions:

  1. Roast semolina.
  2. Temper mustard seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves.
  3. Add onions, green chilies, and vegetables.
  4. Add roasted semolina and water.
  5. Cook until thick.
  6. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Moong dal (split yellow lentils)
  • Ghee
  • Cashews
  • Raisins
  • Ginger
  • Pepper
  • Cumin seeds

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice and moong dal together.
  2. Temper ghee, cashews, raisins, ginger, pepper, and cumin seeds.
  3. Add to the cooked rice and dal.
  4. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • Dosa batter
  • Onions, chopped
  • Tomatoes, chopped
  • Green chilies, chopped
  • Coriander leaves, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Prepare dosa batter.
  2. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and coriander leaves to the batter.
  3. Pour batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve with chutney or sambar.

Ingredients:

  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, drumsticks, beans, etc.)
  • Coconut oil
  • Curd
  • Coconut, grated
  • Green chilies
  • Curry leaves

Instructions:

  1. Cut vegetables into long pieces.
  2. Cook vegetables with coconut oil and curd.
  3. Add grated coconut, green chilies, and curry leaves.
  4. Simmer and serve.

Ingredients:

  • Tamarind extract
  • Tomatoes
  • Rasam powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions:

  1. Extract tamarind pulp.
  2. Add tomatoes and rasam powder.
  3. Boil and simmer.
  4. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  6. Serve hot with rice.
